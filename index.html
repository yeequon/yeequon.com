About

Nothing about my career was planned. I'm told that's normal.

Fifteen years in, mostly in channel and partner programs. What kept me around wasn't the job title. It was the actual work of getting two companies with different goals to agree on something and move together.

Somewhere along the way I got fluent in GTM strategy, vendor alliances, and making a spreadsheet sound genuinely exciting to a room full of executives.

Off the clock, I play guitar in a couple of bands, which is either a great creative outlet or proof I never got over wanting to be a rock star. My degrees are in law and international studies, not marketing, which tells you how well anyone plans this stuff out.
